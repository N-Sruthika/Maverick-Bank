Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers education loans to help students pursue their academic dreams without financial constraints. With competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options, MBI ensures a seamless experience for funding your education.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for students pursuing higher education (UG, PG, or professional courses).
  • Maximum finance: Up to ₹50 lakhs for studies in India and ₹1 crore for overseas studies.
  • Interest rates start at just 8.99% per annum.
  • Hassle-free financing with repayment tenure up to 15 years.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Aadhaar, PAN, Admission Letter, Fee Structure, Parent/Guardian Income Proof.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 18 years (students must have a co-applicant).
  • Applicant must have secured admission to a recognized educational institution.
  • Co-applicant (Parent/Guardian) must have a stable income source.
  • Credit score of the co-applicant: 650+.

Loan amount depends on the course fee, applicant's academic performance, and co-applicant's repayment capacity.

Loan Amount: Up to ₹50 lakhs for studies in India and ₹1 crore for overseas studies.

Example: For a course costing ₹20 lakhs, you can avail up to ₹20 lakhs as a loan (100% coverage).

Minimum Loan Amount: ₹50,000.

Maximum Loan Amount: ₹1 crore (subject to eligibility).

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

Interest Rates: Starting from 8.99% per annum.

Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure ranging from 5 years to 15 years.

Moratorium period: Repayment starts after course completion or 6 months after getting a job, whichever is earlier.

Purpose: Funding tuition fees, hostel fees, books, equipment, travel expenses, and other education-related costs.

Security: No collateral required for loans up to ₹7.5 lakhs. Collateral required for higher amounts.

Special Benefits:

  • No processing fee for loans up to ₹4 lakhs.
  • Flexible repayment options tailored to the student's earning potential.
  • Minimal documentation required.

Documents Required:
