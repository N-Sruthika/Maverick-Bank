FAQs on Gold Loans

1. Who is eligible for a gold loan? +
Individuals aged 18–70 years with valid identification documents.
2. What type of gold is accepted as collateral? +
Gold jewelry, coins, or bars with a minimum purity of 18 karats.
3. What is the maximum loan amount? +
Up to 75% of the market value of the gold pledged.
4. What is the repayment tenure? +
Flexible tenure ranging from 6 months to 3 years.
5. Is a guarantor required? +
No, a guarantor is not mandatory.
6. Can the gold loan be prepaid? +
Yes, prepayment is allowed without any penalty.
7. What is the interest rate? +
Interest rates start at 8.5% per annum, depending on the loan amount and tenure.
8. What documents are required? +
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Proof of residence (e.g., utility bill)
9. What happens if I default on the loan? +
The bank may auction the pledged gold to recover the outstanding amount.
10. Can I redeem my gold early? +
Yes, you can redeem your gold by repaying the loan in full at any time.
