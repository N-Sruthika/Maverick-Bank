FAQs on Personal Loans

1. Who is eligible for a personal loan? +
Individuals aged 21–60 years with a stable income source (salaried or self-employed).
2. What is the minimum and maximum loan amount? +
Minimum ₹50,000 and maximum ₹20 lakhs, depending on eligibility.
3. What is the repayment tenure? +
Flexible tenure ranging from 12 months to 60 months (1–5 years).
4. Is collateral required? +
No, personal loans are unsecured and do not require collateral.
5. What is the interest rate? +
Interest rates start at 10.99% per annum, depending on the applicant's profile and loan amount.
6. Can I prepay the personal loan? +
Yes, prepayment is allowed after completing 6 EMIs without any penalty.
7. What documents are required? +
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Statements (last 6 months)
  • Salary Slips (last 3 months) for salaried individuals
  • IT Returns (last 2 years) for self-employed individuals
8. What is the processing fee? +
A nominal processing fee of up to 2% of the loan amount is applicable.
9. How long does it take to get the loan approved? +
Loan approval and disbursal typically take 1–3 business days after document submission.
10. Can I apply jointly with a co-applicant? +
Yes, you can apply jointly with a co-applicant to increase eligibility.
