Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers home loans to help you build or buy your dream home. With competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options, MBI ensures a seamless experience for fulfilling your housing needs.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for individuals (21+ years).
  • Maximum finance: Up to 90% of the property value.
  • Interest rates start at just 7.99% per annum.
  • Hassle-free financing with repayment tenure up to 30 years.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Aadhaar, PAN, Income Proof, Property Documents, and Bank Statements.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 21 years.
  • Applicant must have a stable income source.
  • Minimum annual income: ₹3 lakhs.
  • Credit score: 700+.

Loan amount depends on the applicant's income, credit score, and property value.

Loan Amount: Up to 90% of the property's market value.

Example: For a property worth ₹50 lakhs, you can avail up to ₹45 lakhs as a loan.

Minimum Loan Amount: ₹5 lakhs.

Maximum Loan Amount: ₹5 crores (subject to eligibility).

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

Interest Rates: Starting from 7.99% per annum.

Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure ranging from 5 years to 30 years.

Option to repay in Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs).

Purpose: Purchase of residential property, construction of a new house, renovation, or extension of an existing property.

Security: Mortgage of the property being purchased.

Special Benefits:

  • No prepayment penalty after 12 months of loan disbursal.
  • Flexible repayment options tailored to your financial needs.
  • Minimal documentation required.

Documents Required:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip/IT Returns)
  • Property Documents
  • Bank Statements (last 6 months)
