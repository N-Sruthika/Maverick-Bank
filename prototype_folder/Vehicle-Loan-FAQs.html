FAQs on Vehicle Loans

1. Who is eligible for a vehicle loan? +
Individuals (employed or self-employed) and organizations operating for at least two years.
2. What type of vehicles can be purchased? +
New two-wheelers & four-wheelers (including electric) and old four-wheelers.
3. What is the minimum down payment? +
  • New Car - 10% on-road price
  • Old Car - 25% on-road price
  • Two-wheeler - 10% on-road price
4. What is the age & income criteria? +
Min age: 18 years, Max age: 70 years (loan must be repaid before 75). Salary slips or IT returns required.
5. Can I refinance my vehicle? +
No, refinance is not available.
6. Can old vehicles be purchased? +
Only old four-wheelers (excluding non-electric) can be financed.
7. What is the repayment tenure? +
  • New car - 84 months
  • Old car - 84 EMI (max 8 years at closure)
  • New two-wheeler - 72 months
8. What is the maximum loan amount? +
90% of on-road price for new vehicles, 75% of market value for old four-wheelers.
9. Is a guarantor mandatory? +
No.
10. Can the vehicle loan be prepaid? +
Yes, prepayment is allowed.
