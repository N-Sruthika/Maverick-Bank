Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers instant personal loans to meet your financial needs without any collateral. With competitive interest rates and quick approval, MBI ensures a seamless experience for fulfilling your personal requirements.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for individuals (21+ years).
  • Maximum finance: Up to ₹50 lakhs.
  • Interest rates start at just 9.99% per annum.
  • Hassle-free financing with repayment tenure up to 5 years.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Aadhaar, PAN, Income Proof, and Bank Statements.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 21 years.
  • Applicant must have a stable income source.
  • Minimum annual income: ₹2 lakhs.
  • Credit score: 650+.

Loan amount depends on the applicant's income, credit score, and repayment capacity.

Loan Amount: Up to ₹50 lakhs.

Example: For an individual earning ₹10 lakhs annually, you can avail up to ₹20 lakhs as a loan.

Minimum Loan Amount: ₹50,000.

Maximum Loan Amount: ₹50 lakhs (subject to eligibility).

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

Interest Rates: Starting from 9.99% per annum.

Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure ranging from 1 year to 5 years.

Option to repay in Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs).

Purpose: Personal expenses such as travel, education, medical emergencies, weddings, or debt consolidation.

Security: No collateral required (Unsecured Loan).

Special Benefits:

  • No prepayment penalty after 6 months of loan disbursal.
  • Flexible repayment options tailored to your financial needs.
  • Minimal documentation required.

Documents Required:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Income Proof (Salary Slip/IT Returns)
  • Bank Statements (last 3 months)
  • Passport-size Photograph
