FAQs on Business Loans

1. Who is eligible for a business loan? +
Businesses operating for at least 2 years, self-employed individuals, and professionals (like doctors, lawyers, etc.) with a minimum annual turnover of ₹5 lakhs.
2. What can I use the business loan for? +
The loan can be used for working capital, business expansion, purchase of equipment, inventory management, or other business-related needs.
3. What is the minimum credit score required? +
A minimum credit score of 750 is required for approval.
4. What is the maximum loan amount? +
Up to ₹50 lakhs for small businesses and up to ₹2 crores for larger enterprises (subject to eligibility).
5. What is the repayment tenure? +
Flexible tenure ranging from 1 year to 5 years, depending on the loan amount and purpose.
6. Is collateral required? +
Collateral is not required for loans up to ₹20 lakhs. For higher amounts, collateral may be necessary.
7. Can I prepay the business loan? +
Yes, prepayment is allowed after 6 months of loan disbursal without any penalty.
8. What documents are required? +
  • PAN Card
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Statements (last 6 months)
  • IT Returns (last 2 years)
  • Business Proof (GST Certificate, Shop Act License, etc.)
9. What is the interest rate? +
Interest rates start at 10.99% per annum, depending on the applicant's profile and loan amount.
10. Can startups apply for a business loan? +
Yes, startups operating for at least 2 years with consistent revenue generation are eligible.
