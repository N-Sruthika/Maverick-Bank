Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers vehicle loan which caters your requirement of driving home your dream car or bike. If you are looking to purchase a new car, a used car, or a two-wheeler, MBI is the right place with the perfect loan solution for you. The bank provides competitive interest rates, making your vehicle purchase affordable in a stress-free atmosphere. MBI ensures a straightforward, convenient, and hassle-free loan process.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for individuals (18+ years).
  • Maximum finance: 90% for new vehicles, 75% for old vehicles.
  • Interest rates start at just 8.85%.
  • Hassle-free financing with up to 7 years repayment for new cars.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Aadhaar, Pan, DL, Salaried person to be in service for 2 years and to produce last three months salary and business person last two years IT returns.

Permanent Employment in a...

  • Government Office
  • Public Sector Undertaking
  • Reputed Private Enterprise
  • Self-employed Professional

Income should be more than Rs. 8000 for car loans and Rs. 5000 for two-wheeler loans.

New Car / New Electric Car: 90% of the cost of the vehicle.

Old Cars: 75% of the market value of the vehicle.

2 Wheelers: 90% of the cost of the vehicle.

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

New Car: Repayable in up to 84 equated monthly installments.

Used Car: Max 84 months, less age of the vehicle.

New Two-Wheeler: Repayable in up to 72 monthly installments.

Purpose: Purchase of New/Old Car (not older than 5 years) and New Two-Wheelers.

Co-applicants: Spouse, Son, Daughter, Father, Mother (Income can be combined).

Security: Hypothecation of the vehicle.

Special Concession:

  • CIBIL Score 750-799: Concession 0.25%.
  • CIBIL Score 800+: Concession 0.50%.

Documents Required:

  • Application form
  • Salary certificate / IT return
  • Quotation/proforma invoice for new vehicles
  • Valuation certificate from two reputed auto dealers for used cars
