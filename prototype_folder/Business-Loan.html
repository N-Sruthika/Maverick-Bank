Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers business loans tailored to meet your entrepreneurial needs. Whether you're expanding your business, purchasing equipment, or managing working capital, MBI provides flexible and competitive loan solutions to help your business grow.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for individuals and businesses.
  • Maximum loan amount up to ₹50 lakhs.
  • Interest rates starting at just 10.50%.
  • Hassle-free financing with repayment tenure up to 5 years.

Q: What documents are required?

A: PAN Card, Aadhaar, GST Certificate, Last 2 years' ITR, Bank Statements (last 6 months), and Proof of Business Existence.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 21 years.
  • Applicant must be a business owner or self-employed professional.
  • Minimum annual turnover: ₹5 lakhs.
  • Credit score: 750+.

Income should be stable and verifiable through IT returns or audited financial statements.

Working Capital Loans: Up to 80% of the total business requirement.

Equipment Financing: Up to 90% of the equipment cost.

Business Expansion: Customized loan amounts based on business needs and creditworthiness.

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

Working Capital Loan: Repayable in up to 36 equated monthly installments.

Equipment Financing: Repayable in up to 60 monthly installments.

Business Expansion Loan: Flexible repayment options based on cash flow.

Purpose: Funding for business expansion, working capital, equipment purchase, or other business-related expenses.

Co-applicants: Spouse, Business Partner, or Family Member (Income can be combined).

Security: Collateral may be required for larger loan amounts.

Special Concession:

  • CIBIL Score 750-799: Concession 0.25%.
  • CIBIL Score 800+: Concession 0.50%.

Documents Required:

  • Application form
  • Last 2 years' IT returns
  • GST Certificate
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
  • Proof of Business Existence
