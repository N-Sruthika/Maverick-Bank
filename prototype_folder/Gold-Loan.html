Maverick Bank of India (MBI) offers instant gold loans against your gold jewelry or ornaments. With minimal documentation and quick approval, you can unlock the value of your gold to meet your financial needs.

Key Highlights:

  • Eligible for individuals (18+ years).
  • Maximum finance: Up to 75% of the gold's market value.
  • Interest rates start at just 7.50%.
  • Hassle-free financing with flexible repayment options.

Q: What documents are required?

A: Aadhaar, PAN, and ownership proof of gold (invoice/bill).

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum age: 18 years.
  • Applicant must own gold jewelry or ornaments.
  • Gold purity: Minimum 18 karats.
  • No restrictions on employment type.

Loan amount depends on the weight and purity of the gold pledged.

Loan Amount: Up to 75% of the appraised value of the gold.

Example: For gold worth ₹1,00,000, you can avail up to ₹75,000 as a loan.

Minimum Loan Amount: ₹10,000.

Maximum Loan Amount: ₹50,00,000 (subject to gold value).

Interest Rates & Repayment Period:

Interest Rates: Starting from 7.50% per annum.

Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure ranging from 6 months to 24 months.

Option to repay in Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs).

Purpose: Personal expenses, medical emergencies, education, business expansion, or any other financial need.

Security: Pledged gold jewelry or ornaments.

Special Benefits:

  • No processing fee for loans above ₹1 lakh.
  • Quick disbursal within 30 minutes.
  • Minimal documentation required.

Documents Required:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Ownership proof of gold (invoice/bill)
  • Passport-size photographs
